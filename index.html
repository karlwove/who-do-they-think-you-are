Meet the Data Brokers

Before we reveal your profile, let's meet the companies that have been watching.

Data brokers are businesses that collect, analyse and sell personal information about consumers. Companies like Acxiom, Experian, and Epsilon maintain profiles on billions of people worldwide.

Here's what they do:
  • Collect data from thousands of sources (apps, websites, purchases, location tracking)
  • Create detailed behavioural profiles with thousands of data points per person
  • Package and sell these profiles to advertisers, employers, insurers and others looking to influence
  • Update profiles in real-time as you browse, shop and move through your day
